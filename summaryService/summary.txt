The text is a compilation of poetry excerpts and an introduction to the 'Poetry Out Loud' program created by the National Endowment for the Arts and the Poetry Foundation. Dana Gioia, the chairman of the National Endowment for the Arts, discusses the purpose of the program, which is to help teachers and students master the art of performing poetry. The program features some of America's best writers and actors reciting their favorite poems and discussing what poetry means to them. The aim is to convey how poetry can potentially change one's life.
